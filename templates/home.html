What are the symptoms of diabetes?

Increased thirst and urination

Excessive thirst and increased urination are common diabetes signs and symptoms. When you have diabetes, excess glucose — a type of sugar — builds up in your blood.

Blurred vision

Blurred vision refers to a lack of sharpness of vision resulting in the inability to see fine detail. Blurred vision may result from abnormalities such as nearsightedness,

Sores that do not heal

Sores that will not heal are a common problem. Open sores that will not heal are also known as wounds or skin ulcers.

Unexplained weight loss

Unexplained weight loss is a noticeable drop in body weight that occurs even if the person is not trying to lose weight. Unexplained weight loss can be a symptom of a serious illness, including cancer or diabetes.
